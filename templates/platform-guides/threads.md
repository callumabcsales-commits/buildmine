# Platform playbook — Threads

**Format:** conversational posts, up to ~500 chars. Replies extend a thought naturally.

**What works on Threads:**
- More casual and human than X. Less "optimized", more "talking to people".
- It reads like a note to peers, not a broadcast. Warmth and candor travel well.
- Storytelling over one-liners — a small arc (I hit this → I felt this → here's where it landed) fits Threads better than a punchy zinger.
- Lower cynicism than X; earnest builds work. Sharing the *feeling* of the work (relief, frustration, the small win) resonates.
- Light on hashtags; they matter less here.

**Hooks that land:**
- The honest open: "ok this one actually got me today."
- The small human moment: "watched the first real user do the thing. sat there grinning."
- The reflective: "learned something the hard way this week."

**Avoid:** copy-pasting the exact X post (rewrite for the room), hard-sell CTAs, over-formatting.

**Respect the voice profile above all** — it overrides these defaults.

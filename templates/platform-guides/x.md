# Platform playbook — X / Twitter

**Format:** single posts and threads. ~280 chars per tweet.

**What works for build-in-public on X:**
- The first line is 90% of the job. Lead with the result, the tension, or a number — never with setup or "So today I…".
- Specificity is the hook. "Cut our cold-start from 4.2s to 300ms" beats "made things faster".
- Show the messy middle. Bugs, dead ends, and the exact fix outperform polished wins.
- One idea per post. If it needs more, make it a thread.
- Threads: tweet 1 is a standalone hook that promises a payoff; each subsequent tweet earns the next; the last delivers the lesson or a soft CTA.

**Hooks that land:**
- The number/result: "Spent 6 hours on a bug that was one missing `await`."
- The contrarian take: "Everyone says X. We did the opposite. Here's what happened."
- The build-in-public honest: "This almost broke me today."
- The before/after: "Yesterday: manual. Today: one command."

**Avoid:** engagement-bait questions, thread-bloat, hashtag spam (0–1 max unless the profile says otherwise), fake vulnerability, corporate tone.

**Respect the voice profile above all** — casing, emoji, and hard rules override these defaults.

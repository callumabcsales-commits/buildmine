# Platform playbook — Instagram (reel ideas + carousels)

buildmine produces a **reel idea**, not a finished video. The user can expand any idea into a full shot-by-shot video with `/buildmine-reel` (which hands off to Seedance).

For stories with a beat-by-beat arc — a struggle→fix→lesson, a step-by-step tool setup, a ranked list — also offer a **carousel outline**. Carousel rules: slide 1 is the hook and nothing else (it does 90% of the job), one thought per slide, 6–10 slides, penultimate slide is the payoff, last slide is a soft CTA, big readable type, identical design every slide. When the user approves an outline, the `buildmine-carousel` skill renders it **in their brand** (from `.claude/buildmine/brand-profile.md`) as 1080×1350 PNG slides ready to upload.

**A reel idea must contain:**
- **Hook (first ~1.5s):** the single on-screen line + visual that stops the thumb. This is do-or-die on Reels.
- **Concept (15–30s):** what's shown / what happens. Keep it producible by a solo founder — screen recordings, talking head, text-on-motion, quick b-roll.
- **On-screen text beats:** timed captions (people watch muted). Each beat advances the story.
- **Caption:** in the user's voice; front-load the hook since IG truncates; hashtags per the voice profile (IG tolerates more than X/Threads, but keep them relevant).

**What works for build-in-public reels:**
- Fast, muted-friendly, text-driven. Assume no sound.
- Screen-recording the actual thing working is gold ("watch this go from broken to working").
- Before/after and time-lapse formats perform.
- A clear payoff in the first 3 seconds or they scroll.

**Formats that fit a founder:**
- Screen-record + on-screen narration of a fix or feature.
- "POV: you finally squashed the bug that ate your week."
- Build-in-public metric reveal (only real numbers).
- Talking head over b-roll of the product.

**Avoid:** slow intros, tiny unreadable text, fake urgency, unreal metrics.

**Respect the voice profile above all** — it overrides these defaults.
